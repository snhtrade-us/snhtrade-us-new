Strategic Market
Positioning

CEO: Jeffrey Han (Ju Hee Han)

S&H Global Trade Corp. leverages extensive expertise in international trade to connect premium suppliers with North American markets. We operate as a "Profit Center" for our partners, ensuring a robust supply chain, meticulous quality assurance, and comprehensive resolution of all distribution-related claims.

Our core mission is to establish a sophisticated franchise-style supply network that introduces high-quality consumer products adapted to current U.S. market trends.

Growth & Evolution

2014 - Establishment

Formal entry into the trade industry. Built the foundational infrastructure for specialized import and distribution services within the United States.

2017 - Trend Adaptation

Initiated data-driven identification of emerging consumer trends. Signed technical cooperation agreements to diversify business consulting and service offerings.

2020 - Operational Excellence

Established advanced CRM and inventory systems for prompt client response, significantly enhancing customer service and logistical reliability.

2023 - 2026 Global Reach

Strategic expansion into international markets, increasing global operational range and stabilizing the diversified trade portfolio.
