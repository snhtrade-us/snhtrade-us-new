Positioning & Vision

CEO: Jeffrey Han (Ju Hee Han)

S&H Global Trading Corp. serves as a specialized import and distribution partner, prioritizing the profitability of our clients. We function as a 'Profit Center' that takes full responsibility for quality assurance and the resolution of all post-distribution claims.

Our Vision: To become a premier franchise-style supply chain that identifies and introduces the latest consumer trends to the North American market through robust distribution networks.

Our Journey of Growth

2014 - Establishment

Founding of S&H Global Trade Corp. and entry into the North American trade industry, laying the foundation for an integrated import/export infrastructure.

2017 - Strategic Diversification

Commenced identifying emerging consumer trends and signed technical cooperation agreements to expand business consulting services.

2020 - CRM & Efficiency

Established advanced systems for prompt client response and automated inventory tracking, significantly enhancing customer service capabilities.

2023 ~ 2026 - Global Reach

Strategic expansion into international markets and stabilization of the global trade portfolio, ensuring long-term sustainable growth.

Detailed Operational Strategy

Eco-Friendly Food

  • (a) Sourcing natural ingredients and Home Meal Replacements (HMR) with strong price competitiveness.
  • (b) Expanding product lines centered on health-conscious trends in the U.S. consumer market.
  • (c) Optimizing logistics and shelf-life management systems to maintain peak freshness.

Beauty & Lifestyle

  • (a) Building a lineup of functional cosmetics that meet global safety standards (FDA, etc.).
  • (b) Supplying trendy, high-durability household goods and office supplies.
  • (c) Eliminating intermediate margins through direct-import structures to gain price leadership.

IT & Consumer Electronics

  • (a) Providing a stable supply of high-performance PC peripherals and office consumables.
  • (b) Introducing smart gadgets and lifestyle security products to diversify our portfolio.
  • (c) Securing priority access to latest-spec products through technical partnerships.
